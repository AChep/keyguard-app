Almost everything about how Keyguard looks lives in one place: **Settings →
Appearance**.

## Theme & color

- **Theme** — *Follow system settings*, *Light*, or *Dark*. Following the
  system flips with your OS day/night setting.
- **Use contrast black theme** — an AMOLED-friendly variant that paints dark
  backgrounds pure black, saving power on OLED screens. Only matters while a
  dark theme is active.
- **Use expressive theme** — opts into the more playful Material 3 *expressive* styling.
- **Tint color** — the accent applied across the app. Choose *Follow system
  settings* to track your device's dynamic color, or pick one of the built-in
  swatches (Red, Pink, Purple, Blue, Teal, Green, Amber, Orange, and more).
- **Font** — *Follow system settings*, *Roboto*, *Noto*, or *Atkinson
  Hyperlegible* — a typeface designed to increase legibility for readers with
  low vision.

## Icons & avatars

- **Load App icons** — show the real launcher icon for items matched to an
  installed app, instead of a generic placeholder.
- **Load Website icons** — fetch favicons for login URLs so each item carries
  its site's logo.
- **Load Gravatar icons** — show a Gravatar avatar for items with an email
  address.

> **Privacy trade-off.** Website icons and Gravatar avatars are fetched **over
> the network**, which can reveal the sites in your vault to your internet
> provider. Turn these off if that matters more to you than the visuals.

## Layout

Keyguard can show the list and the open item side by side instead of as two
separate screens. The two toggles let you decide per orientation:

- **Allow two panel layout in portrait mode**
- **Allow two panel layout in landscape mode**

Both kick in only when the window is wide enough to fit two panels, so they
mostly affect tablets and desktop windows.

## Behavior

- **Open links in external browser** *(Android)* — opens URLs in your default
  browser app rather than an in-app browser tab.
- **Keep the screen on while viewing items** — prevents the screen from dimming
  off while an item is open.
- **Minimize on copy** *(Desktop)* — hides the window after you copy a value.
- **Close to system tray** *(Desktop)* — closing the window tucks Keyguard into
  the tray instead of quitting it.
- **Rich text formatting** — render Markdown in notes rather than showing the raw text.
