Pull your vault out on demand, whether that means a handful of individual items or an entire organization's collection. Exports can optionally include the attachments.

<Screenshot src={exportAccount} caption="An account's export options — export individual items or the entire vault." />

The point is that you own your data. Your credentials, notes, and the files attached to them remain yours to take elsewhere at any time, with no lock-in keeping them trapped inside one app. Move to another tool, archive a copy for safekeeping, or hand off an organization's data when ownership changes.
Whatever the reason, the data leaves on your terms and in full.
