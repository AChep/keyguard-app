Choose how you reopen your vault after it locks. You can type your password, use the device biometrics such as a fingerprint or face scan, or tap a hardware YubiKey.

<Screenshot src={unlockScreen} caption="The vault unlock screen offering a password field alongside a biometric prompt and a YubiKey option." />

## Your balance of convenience and security

Biometrics make daily access quick, while a password or a YubiKey adds a stronger gate when you want it. The decision stays with you, and you can lean toward speed or toward caution depending on the situation.
