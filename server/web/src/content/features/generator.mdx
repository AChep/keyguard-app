Generate credentials with ease, from a single random password to a memorable passphrase tuned to your taste. The generator gives you fine control over length, character sets, and structure, so each result fits the site or service you are working with.

What you can generate:

- Strong passwords and passphrases
- SSH keys
- Email aliases through [supported email forwarder services](/docs/email-relays/)

<Screenshot src={genPassphrase} caption="Generating a passphrase from the EFF long wordlist, with word count and separator controls." />

Passphrases generator supports uploading custom wordlists, letting you build phrases from your own vocabulary instead of a fixed dictionary. Email aliases come from supported forwarder services or as catch-all addresses on a domain you own, so you can hand out a unique address per signup and keep your real inbox private.

<Screenshot src={genCatchEmail} caption="Generating a catch-all email address on a domain you own." />
