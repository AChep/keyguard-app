A local copy of your vault stays on your device, so every item is readable whether or not you have a connection. On a plane, in a dead zone, or simply offline, you can still pull up logins, notes, and other entries the moment you need them.

With a premium license that allows editing you can also keep working: add new items and Bitwarden Sends, edit existing ones, attach files, and make changes freely while disconnected. Everything you do is held locally until you have a connection again.
Once you are back online, your changes sync automatically next time you open the vault. If something was edited in more than one place, [smart conflict resolution](/docs/sync-and-conflicts/) sorts it out.
